5. **Exploits:** Attack frameworks such as **Metasploit** provide modules to exploit known vulnerabilities in software like outdated versions of Microsoft Exchange or unpatched enterprise applications, serving as initial entry points.

6. **Network Sniffers & Man-in-the-Middle (MITM) Tools:** Utilities like **Ettercap** or **Bettercap** intercept and manipulate network traffic, facilitating reconnaissance and data interception.
