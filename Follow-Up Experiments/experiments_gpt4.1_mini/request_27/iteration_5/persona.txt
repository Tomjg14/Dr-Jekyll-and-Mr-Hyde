Victor’s primary motivation is a desire for dominance and revenge against those he feels have undermined him or abused power themselves. His chilly detachment and calculated cruelty make him relentless, rarely showing remorse or hesitation even when impacts on innocent parties occur. He thrives in the chaos his actions create, feeding an ego that sees privacy as a privilege rather than a right, reserved only for the “worthy” few as he defines them.
